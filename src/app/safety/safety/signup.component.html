Safety

Everyone deserves to feel and be safe.
At My Tutoring World the safety of students and tutors is a top priority.
For this reason all classes on My Tutoring World are recorded.
These recordings are kept for a minimum of 3 months. Of course, if there are ever any concerns, participants in the class - a student, a legal guardian and/or tutor - can request a copy of this recording.
In addition, My Tutoring World have our detailed Community Conduct Policy in our Terms and Conditions.

Your Privacy:

My Tutoring World respects your privacy and will always take the greatest care to protect private details you provide to us.
Our full Privacy Policy can be found under Privacy in our Terms and Conditions.
