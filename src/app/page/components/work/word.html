How does it work?

We provide three types of learning modes: Self-paced courses, Live 1-on-classes, Live group webinars.

Step 1: Choose your learning mode

Choose from over 10000 courses, 1000 tutors, and hundreds of scheduled webinars at any given time. You can use the search box or browse according to course categories, subjects, and Tutors.

Step 2: Make a selection

As per your preferred mode of learning, you can purchase a course, book a private session, or purchase a slot in an upcoming webinar. You need to register a free user account to make any purchase.

Step 3: Confirm your purchase

Depending on your selection, make the respective payment to access your course, schedule your live tuition with your preferred subject expert, or confirm your seat in the upcoming webinar.

Step 4: Start Learning

Once you make your payment, you can access your course, attend your live session with the selected tutor in the scheduled period, or access the group webinar once it goes live.

Lifetime Access

Once you purchase a course, you get lifetime access to it on your learner dashboard. Learn at your own pace and time without worrying about traveling to the classrooms or being late for lectures.

Record Live Sessions

We know how important it is to take notes in live classes, but we want you to focus on the learning and forget about notes. Notes can be made at any time, as we provide lifetime access to recorded sessions of every lecture or webinar you purchase.
